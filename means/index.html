About Statistical Means:

  • Arithmetic Mean (AM): Simple average of values. Gives equal weight to each value.
  • Geometric Mean (GM): Represents the central tendency of a set of numbers by using their product. Useful when values have different ranges.
  • Harmonic Mean (HM): Reciprocal of the arithmetic mean of the reciprocals. Used when dealing with rates or ratios.
  • Quadratic Mean (QM): Also known as Root Mean Square (RMS). Emphasizes larger values more than smaller ones.

Relationships:

For any two positive numbers: HM ≤ GM ≤ AM ≤ QM

This means: Harmonic Mean ≤ Geometric Mean ≤ Arithmetic Mean ≤ Quadratic Mean
