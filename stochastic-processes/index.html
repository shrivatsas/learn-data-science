About Stochastic Processes

A stochastic process is a collection of random variables indexed by time. Explore different processes above to understand their unique properties:

  • Random Walk: Sum of independent random steps
  • Brownian Motion: Continuous-time limit of random walk
  • Markov Chain: Memory-less discrete state process
  • Poisson Process: Counting process for rare events
  • Lévy Process: Heavy-tailed jumps with stable distributions
  • Gaussian Process: Smooth functions with specified covariance
